Our Story

Woven from Generations of Craftsmanship

Tanvi by Lakshya was born from a simple belief: that Kerala's textile and dance traditions deserve to be carried forward with the same reverence they were created with. Every Set Mundu, every Kasavu border, and every temple ornament is sourced directly from master artisans.

We work closely with handloom clusters in Balaramapuram and goldsmiths who practice techniques passed down through centuries, bringing you pieces that are as authentic as they are exquisite.
