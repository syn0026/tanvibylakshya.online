പൈതൃകം · Our Story

Woven from Generations of Craftsmanship

Tanvi by Lakshya was born from a simple belief: that Kerala's textile and dance traditions deserve to be carried forward with the same reverence they were created with. Every Set Mundu, every Kasavu border, every temple ornament in our collection is sourced directly from master artisans across Kerala's weaving villages.

We work closely with handloom clusters in Balaramapuram and Kuthampully, and with goldsmiths who still practice the temple jewellery techniques passed down through their families, to bring you pieces that are as authentic as they are exquisite.
